Items need to confirm with Shri:

 * Are we just concentrating on Mac for MVP, or Windows too? - Both, but more
   emphasis on Mac.

 * Do we need 2 sets of UI for Mac and Windows, or can we have it more
   universal? - Universal would be better, but in areas where it would be too
   boring, we should consider OS-specific UI.

Below, "Wishlist MVP" means it would be nice to have for the MVP, but at least
have it in mind during design so that the product is designed for smooth
integration when the feature finally comes. So, design for MVP, though it may
not get into the MVP product release.
